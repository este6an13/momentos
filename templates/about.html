This gallery is a personal project, a place to share photos I like.

I wanted a feed I can adjust over time: editing details, changing descriptions, and reordering images. Visitors can view the photos chronologically or at random, and search the gallery using keywords, making it easier to explore the collection at their own pace; things that aren’t possible on platforms like Instagram, or VSCO.

I also wanted to post without bothering people or occupying their feeds. This is a place meant to be visited intentionally. There’s no algorithm deciding what should be seen, no pressure to post regularly, and no need to compete for attention.

Just a quiet, personal collection, open to anyone who chooses to look.
